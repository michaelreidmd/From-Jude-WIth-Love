About Jude

A baker shaped by care, discipline, and home

Jude is a mother of three, a trained midwife, a regular 5K runner, and proudly Home Counties born. What began as a quiet kitchen ritual during Covid grew into a way of feeding neighbours, teaching others, and making something deeply real by hand.

Her baking carries the same qualities that run through the rest of her life: steadiness, warmth, attention to detail, and a real love of nourishing people well.
